About

My work is grounded in clarity, shaped by curiosity, and driven by the why behind what we build.

I grew up in Houston and earned a B.S. in Industrial Design from the University of Houston, with a minor in Architecture. I started in physical product — housewares, exhibits, prototypes — before moving into digital product and UX.

When I'm not designing, you'll usually find me working on cars — fixing anything from a flat tire to a blown piston.
